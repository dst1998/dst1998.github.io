This computer architecture project implemented a 5-stage pipelined 32-bit MIPS processor in Verilog, including registers and an arithmetic-logic unit for instruction-memory and data-memory access across R-, I-, and J-type instructions.

The project synthesized the design with Synopsys Design Compiler and optimized a 0.13 um ALU using a carry-lookahead adder, output selection, and logic reconfiguration, improving area and power by approximately 30%.
