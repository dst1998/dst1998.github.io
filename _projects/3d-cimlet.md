3D-CIMlet is a heterogeneous chiplet framework for in-memory acceleration of edge LLM inference and continual learning. The project combines thermal-aware modeling, multi-die design exploration, and model-to-architecture mapping across 2.5D and 3D systems.

Key contributions include memory-reliability-aware chiplet mapping for heterogeneous RRAM, eDRAM, and hybrid dies in mixed technology nodes; compute-storage allocation; chiplet interconnect optimization; and chiplet-to-package thermal analysis.

The framework achieved 9.3x/12x energy efficiency improvement and 90.2%/92.5% EDP reduction in 2.5D/3D designs.
