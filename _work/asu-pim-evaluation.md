Developed the PIMA-SIM processing-in-memory evaluation tool on top of NVSIM and improved PIM-level configuration flexibility. Evaluated high-density 28 nm STT-assisted SOT-MRAM, achieving 95.4% lower energy, 1.6× speedup, and 65% lower area overhead compared with SRAM.
